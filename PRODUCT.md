# PawHub — Pet Care & Pet Shop Management Platform

## Product
All-in-one digital hub for pet owners and pet shop businesses. One platform replaces multiple fragmented services.

## Users
- **Pet owners**: buy pet products, book vet appointments, reserve pet hotel, use AI pet assistant
- **Business owners/admins**: manage products, vet schedules, hotel rooms, track bookings, view sales reports

## Core Features
### Customer Side
- Browse & purchase pet products (food, toys, accessories, health supplies)
- Search by pet type, category, brand
- Schedule veterinary appointments; view vets and services
- Book pet hotel stays (boarding and daycare)
- Manage pet profiles (name, breed, age, medical info)
- View order history and booking history
- Appointment and reservation reminders

### AI Pet Assistant (24/7 chatbot)
- Answer product questions and recommend by pet type/breed/age
- Explain vet services and procedures
- Help book appointments and hotel stays
- Provide pet care tips
- Track bookings and orders
- Escalate to staff when needed

### Admin/Business Side
- Manage products and inventory
- Manage vet services and schedules
- Manage hotel rooms/cages and availability
- Track all bookings and appointments
- Sales and revenue reports
- Manage customer info and pet profiles
- Review chatbot conversations

## Brand
Warm, friendly, modern, trustworthy. Centered on pet wellbeing.
