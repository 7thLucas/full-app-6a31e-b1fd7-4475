# PawHub — Product Overview

## Product Name
**PawHub**

## One-Line Positioning
All-in-one pet care platform — shop pet products, book vet appointments, reserve hotel stays, and get AI-powered guidance in a single account.

## The Problem
Pet owners juggle multiple separate services: a pet shop website for food and supplies, a standalone vet booking system, a boarding/hotel service for holidays, and scattered online searches for pet care advice. Every need means a new login, a new workflow, and no shared record of their pet's history.

## The Solution
PawHub unifies the entire pet care journey into one platform. Pet owners handle every need — buying, booking, boarding, and asking — without switching apps, re-entering pet details, or losing track of history. Pet shop businesses get a single operations hub to manage it all.

## Users / Personas

### Pet Owner (Customer)
- Browse and purchase pet products (food, toys, accessories, health supplies)
- Search products by pet type, category, and brand
- Schedule veterinary appointments; view available vets and services
- Book pet hotel stays for boarding and daycare
- Manage pet profiles (name, breed, age, medical information)
- View order history and booking history
- Receive reminders for upcoming appointments and hotel reservations
- Chat with the AI Pet Assistant 24/7

### Business Owner / Admin
- Manage products and inventory
- Manage veterinary services and schedules
- Manage hotel rooms, cages, and availability
- Track all bookings and appointments
- View sales and revenue reports
- Manage customer information and pet profiles
- Review AI chatbot conversations and common questions

## AI Pet Assistant
Available 24/7. Capabilities:
- Answer product questions and recommend by pet type, breed, age, and needs
- Explain veterinary services and procedures
- Help customers book appointments and hotel stays
- Provide basic pet care tips and recommendations
- Help customers track bookings and orders
- Escalate complex questions to staff when necessary

## Brand & Tone
- **Feel**: Warm, friendly, modern, trustworthy
- **Audience tone**: Caring — centered on pet wellbeing, not clinical or transactional
- **Design**: Clean card-based layouts, mobile-first, easy navigation across Shop / Vet / Hotel / AI
- **Colors**: Warm orange primary, teal secondary, soft green wellness accents

## Scope (MVP)
- Customer-facing: product shop, vet booking, hotel reservation, pet profiles, AI assistant, history & reminders
- Admin-facing: inventory, vet schedule, hotel room management, booking tracking, sales reports, chatbot review
- Auth: login/register with role-based access control (pet owner vs. admin)

## Strategic Principles
1. One account, all care — no re-entering pet data across services
2. Business and customer sides in the same platform — no separate tools
3. AI assistant reduces staff inquiry load while improving customer response time
4. Warm and professional — appeals to pet lovers without feeling like a vet clinic
