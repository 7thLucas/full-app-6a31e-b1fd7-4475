The Foundation

Why PawHub exists

✕

The Problem

📱

Multiple apps, multiple logins

Pet shop, vet system, boarding service — each a separate account with separate workflows.

📋

Pet data re-entered everywhere

Breed, age, medical history — typed again at every service, with no shared record.

🕐

No instant answers, no reminders

Owners call or wait for replies to basic questions about products, services, or bookings.

📊

Businesses manage across tools

Sales, bookings, inventory, and schedules tracked in disconnected spreadsheets and systems.

✓

The Solution

🏠

One platform, every need

Shop, vet, hotel, and AI assistant in a single account — no app-switching required.

🐾

Shared pet profiles

Name, breed, age, and medical info stored once — used across every service automatically.

🤖

AI assistant available 24/7

Answers questions, recommends products, helps book services, and escalates when needed.

📈

Unified admin dashboard

Products, vet schedules, hotel rooms, bookings, and revenue — one view, one system.
